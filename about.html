About Me

Hi, I’m Bibek Ray.
Data Scientist. Builder. Sketcher of both models and meaning.
I’m based in Bangalore, where I work at the intersection of machine learning, full-stack development, and thoughtful design. My projects range from predictive modeling and internal tooling to hackathon prototypes and late-night UI experiments.
I’m currently diving deeper into transformer architectures and attention mechanisms—not for the buzzwords, but to understand what’s actually going on under the hood.
This blog is where I think out loud. Some of it is technical, some personal, and some just doesn’t fit anywhere else.
Views are my own. Especially the controversial ones.
