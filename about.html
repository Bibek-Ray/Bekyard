About Me

Hi, I’m Bibek Ray.
Data Scientist. Marvel Fanatic. Gamer. Sketcher.
I’m based in Bangalore, working as a Data Scientist at Tredence Inc. My core expertise lies at the intersection of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and full-stack development. I've worked on LLM agents, built an inventory management system, and even dabbled in game development.
Right now, I’m diving deeper into transformer architectures and attention mechanisms—not for the buzzwords, but to genuinely understand what’s going on under the hood.
This blog is where I think out loud. Some posts are technical, some personal, and some just... don’t fit anywhere else.
Views are my own. Especially the controversial ones.
