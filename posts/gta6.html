GTA 6

The neon glow of Vice City returns — but this time, it's personal.

Lucia and Jason, our dual protagonists, aren’t just new faces. They’re a storm. The teaser already screams "heat, heist, heartbreak" — all wrapped in a synthwave sunset.

What excites me the most?

  • 🕶️ Return of Vice City — pastel paradise reimagined
  • 💥 Dual-character narrative with Bonnie & Clyde vibes
  • 🚗 High-speed chases that feel like Fast & Furious meets Heat
  • 📱 Potential for modern tech (TikTok missions??)

Rockstar’s silence has been louder than most games' hype campaigns — and with the visuals this slick, they didn’t need to say a word.

"We ain’t just playing the game. We’re living in it." — probably Jason, probably during a 5-star police chase.
